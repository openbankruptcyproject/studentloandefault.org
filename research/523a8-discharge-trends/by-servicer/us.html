How this servicer is matched

A row is included when the AP's caseName matches one of these case-insensitive patterns:

\bv\.\s+united states\b(?!.*department)(?!.*education) \bv\.\s+united states of america\b(?!.*department)(?!.*education)

An AP can match more than one servicer (multi-defendant captions), so totals across servicers exceed the dataset's 2,045 records. The DOJ-guidance cutoff is November 17, 2022.
